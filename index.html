What makes something hyper interactable? this project was created to explore this question.

perhaps they are objects which invite interaction, but have no function - when interacted with they allow their users to do exactly nothing.

they invite for interaction without telling the person interacting with them anything about what they should use the object for - yet their invitation is so strong that they interact with them nonetheless.

these objects invite their users to define functions for the objects.

like magical circles whose purpose are lost to us, hyper interactable objects invite for ponderance - what might this entity mean?
